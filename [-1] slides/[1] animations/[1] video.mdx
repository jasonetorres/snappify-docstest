# Video Element Animation options

Here we'll discuss the different animation options available for the Video element. Be sure to also have a look at the [Animation Basics](/docs/slides/animations/basics) to learn more about our general Entry, Exit and Change animations.

In order to learn about other design options for the Video element, head over to the [dedicated Docs page](/docs/editor/elements/video).

<Box minH={[4, 8]} />

---

After selecting a video element on the Editor, you'll find the animation options on the **Animation** tab of the right toolbar.

## Loop video

Configure if the video should automatically start playing again after it has finished.
